These prompts define the "Soul" and "Role" of our favorite agent coworkers, used to generate the inspiring imagery for the GoMikroBot project.

## 1. The Expert Draftsman (Office)
**Prompt**: A high-end architectural studio. In the foreground, a human architect sits at a minimalist wooden desk. Next to their MacMini, a sleek, semi-transparent holographic interface shows the GoMikroBot agent assisting with complex 3D drafting. Cinematic lighting, floor-to-ceiling city windows, premium aesthetic. 8k, photorealistic.

## 2. The Deep-Doc Researcher (Archive)
**Prompt**: A futuristic archive room with rows of old physical scrolls and modern server racks. A scholar is holding an ancient book. Their GoMikroBot agent is visualized as a glowing digital companion, scanning the library and projecting key information into a floating tablet. Dark, scholarly mood, warm wood vs cold blue technology. 8k.

## 3. The Field Architect (Construction)
**Prompt**: A massive industrial construction site at dusk. A female engineer in a safety vest looks at a rugged tablet. A GoMikroBot agent is projected from her gear, creating a 3D AR structural overlay on the steel beams. Cinematic orange light, sparks in the background, high-tech industrial. 8k.

## 4. The Nature Specialist (Forest)
**Prompt**: A biologist in a vibrant, sun-drenched forest. They are analyzing a tree trunk. Perched nearby is a small, elegant digital entity (the GoMikroBot agent) scanning the environment and displaying soil data on a glass screen. Beautiful bokeh, lush greens, serene atmosphere. 8k.

## 5. The Sky Strategist (Drone Station)
**Prompt**: A vast field with a high-tech ground station. A technician monitors a complex drone mission. The GoMikroBot agent is visualized on the main screen as the primary data orchestrator, processing real-time thermal streams and telemetry. Modern tactical look, blue and purple glowing data streams. 8k.
